    24.171 CZK/EUR

  69.53 CZK/m3 / 24.171 CZK/EUR = 2.87617 EUR/m3
  2.87617 EUR/m3 / 1000 kg/m3 = 0.00287617 EUR/kg

Notes:
  Only the potable-water supply tariff (vodne) is included. The sewerage
  tariff (stocne) is excluded because water used by electrolysis is consumed
  in the process and is not assumed to be discharged to the sewer system.
